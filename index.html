A little about us

Coffee, Tea & Good Vibes

Pewdys Cafe was born from a love of soft art, warm cups, and the dreamy feeling of floating on a cloud. We're a modern cafe designed to be your place of calm, relaxation and escape from the everyday hustle.

The cloud isn't just our mascot, it's our philosophy. Every drink gets a cloud of foam, every corner gets a little softness, every guest gets a warm welcome.

We also believe in the little moments of stillness. Pewdys is a gentle space to breathe, reset and reconnect with yourself, whether that's through a nice coffee, an open journal, or simply just being.

We take our tea seriously too. From delicate Taiwanese oolongs to earthy Pu'er and soothing herbal blends, tea has been a cornerstone of wellness and culture across Asia for centuries. We bring those traditions to your cup with care, so you can taste the difference.

And yes, we believe great coffee and tea should not cost you a fortune. Consider Pewdys your quiet stand against overpriced cafe culture, big flavour, honest prices, zero regrets.
